# Mission Merch Studio

Mission Merch Studio is a Christian manual-fulfilment custom merchandise platform for churches, missionaries, charities, ministries, and Christian events.

## Positioning

**Custom Christian merch for churches, missions, and ministries.**

Design online, submit your order, and we handle artwork review, production, and delivery.

## First MVP

The first version should allow a church, mission, charity, or ministry to:

1. Choose a product.
2. Upload artwork or logo.
3. Add text/scripture.
4. Preview the design.
5. Submit an order.
6. Pay through Stripe later.
7. Wait for artwork review.
8. Receive production and delivery updates.

## Manual fulfilment first

No Gelato integration in MVP. Orders are reviewed and fulfilled manually by the platform owner.
